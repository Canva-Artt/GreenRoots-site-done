How to Use GreenRoots Hair Oil

Achieve healthier, stronger hair with the right application! Follow these simple steps for the best results:

  • Take a small amount of GreenRoots Oil in your palms.
  • Massage gently into your scalp with fingertips in circular motions.
  • Apply the remaining oil along the length of your hair.
  • Leave it overnight (at least 3 times a week).
  • Wash with a mild, sulfate-free shampoo the next morning.
  • Use regularly to improve hair strength, shine, and overall health.
