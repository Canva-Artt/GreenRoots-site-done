How to Use GreenRoots Hair Oil

Transform your hair care routine with these simple application steps for maximum benefits:

  • Step 1: Take a small amount (about 1-2 teaspoons) of GreenRoots Oil in your palms and warm it by rubbing your hands together.
  • Step 2: Massage gently into your scalp with fingertips in circular motions for 5-7 minutes to stimulate blood circulation.
  • Step 3: Apply the remaining oil along the length of your hair, paying special attention to the tips which are often most damaged.
  • Step 4: Leave it overnight for best results (at least 3 times a week) to allow deep penetration of nutrients.
  • Step 5: Wash with a mild, sulfate-free shampoo the next morning. For extremely dry hair, a second wash may be needed.
  • Step 6: Use regularly to improve hair strength, shine, and overall health. Results typically appear within 2-3 weeks of consistent use.
